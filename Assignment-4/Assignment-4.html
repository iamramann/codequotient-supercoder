Your Paneer Kohlapuri Recipe

A typical Maharashtrian taste profile, with very rich, complex spice notes, perfectly balanced with chilli and fried coconut. Paneer Kolhapuri is great with a crispy parantha or steamed rice, along with some amti dal or kachumber.

Total time: 50-55 minutes

Ingredients

  • Coconut: 1 cup (70 gms approx.),grated
  • Dry Red Chillies: 4 pieces
  • Onion: 2, finely chopped
  • Paneer: 750gms,cut into cubes
  • Caesar Dressing

Preparation

  1. In a karahi, dry roast grated coconut, cloves, cinnamon, whole pepper, red chillies, poppy seeds, coriander seeds and aniseed, till coconut is golden brown. Cool and grind coarsely.

    Time: 25 minutes

  2. Heat oil in a karahi and sauté the onions till soft and translucent, add ginger-garlic paste and fry till the raw smell is gone. Add the tomatoes and salt and cook till the tomatoes are done and the oil begins to separate.

    Time: 10 minutes

  3. Add the coarsely ground spices, mix well and fry for 5-10 minutes. Add water, paneer and MAGGI Masala-ae-Magic. Cook on low flame for 8-10 minutes after.

    Time: 20 minutes
